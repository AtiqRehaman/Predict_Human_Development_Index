Human Development Index

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a composite statistic measuring life expectancy, education, and per capita income indicators. It ranks countries into tiers of human development.

Our advanced machine learning model analyzes socioeconomic components to dynamically classify and predict regional developments. By considering life expectancy, educational attainment, and gross national income, we provide accurate HDI classifications for regions worldwide.
